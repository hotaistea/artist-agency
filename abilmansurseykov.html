Абильмансур Сериков

Serikov Abilmansur – Kazakh actor of cinema and theatre.
Laureate of the “Daryn” State Youth Award of the Government of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

Born in 1991, Almaty, Kazakhstan.

The same year, he entered the Kazakh National Academy of Arts named after T.K. Zhurgenov, Theatre Faculty (course led by Honored Artist of the Kazakh SSR, Professor Rakhilyam Mashurova). Graduated in 2013.

Experience:

  • 2013–2015 – Actor at the Kazakh State Academic Theatre for Children and Youth named after G. Musrepov (Almaty)
  • Since 2015 – Actor at the Kazakh State Musical Drama Theatre named after K. Kuanyshbayev (Astana)

Took part in numerous theatre productions and films in Kazakhstan.
